Reality


I'm Not Okay...

I'm not okay, but I will be okay..
Because I am holding on to an ember of hope,
enough to shine on my feet to make sure
I won't fall into a hole.

I'm not okay, but I will be okay..
Don't ask me what I'll do next week,
I'm just trying to get through today with a smile
instead of tears.

I'm not okay, but I will be okay..
I am joyful for the friendly smiles of strangers,
grateful for the hugs from dear friends,
thankful for the love and kisses from loved ones.

I'm not okay, but I will be okay..
I got a whole lung full of air that
I can breathe and showers of love and care.

I'm not okay, but I will be okay..
Because tomorrow is another day of life to be thankful for.

I'm not okay, but I will be okay..
Because I have so much more to learn, so much more love to give.

I'm not okay, but I will be okay..
Life might try to beat, kick and smack me to the ground,
but I will get up again.
Bruised, battered and bloodied I may be
but I will keep taking another step and walking my path.

I'm not okay, but I will be okay..
I won't give up or give in, because I was brought to this life
for a reason and purpose,
and I will fulfill that.
