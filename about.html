    Here's a bit about what we're trying to accomplish here --our purpose-- and who's behind this site.


    Our Purpose is 3 fold:

    1. It's about helping you discover (really uncover) the reason you're on this planet (Hint: It's usually closer at hand than we ever imagine...)

    2. Then we help you understand the evironment --the mountain you'll be climbing-- which is the 21st century.

    3. And lastly is the practical, rubber-meets-the-road, knowledge and skills acquisition that is often the missing ingredient -- the most essential aspect of preparation to ensure success.


    Now, let's drill deeper...

    First, it's about Hope. Finding your purpose, helps you find yourself -- where you're going in life. And that brings hope like nothing else. If you have ever been lost, you know what I mean. Because..

    Once you know where you're going in life, you have clarity. And that is true hope.

    Let's call this - your destination. Like when you go traveling. This is where you want to end up.


    Second, we aim to help you better understand how our world has and is continuing to to change, morph, upgrade and evolve. (Hint, technology is at the heart of the matter).

    Don't worry. This does NOT mean you need to become a geek. But, we all need to understand how techology is reconfiguring the way we live and work. And we need to master if we're going to pursue our purpose.

    Let's call part ii, the map. With this we have the lay of the land. And we understand the territory we'll be passing through on the way to our destination.


    Third, it's great to know where you want to go and to have a map. But then there's making a decision,
    [Yes! I'm going to do it.], and preparing... and most importantly setting out on our journey.

    So, the third part of this site's mission is to help you prepare for your journey. But not in some superficial, haphazard, happy- go-lucky or foolish manner. Rather we aim to get you fully equiped with the tools, skills and strategies to succeed.

    Take for instance if you wanted to climb a mountain, say Mount Everest. It's one thing to have that as a pipe dream (What most people do...), but once you've decided to climb that mountain, it's time to start preparing.

    You'll need to get in shape physically. Then, you'll need equipment for the climb and training. I mean if you've never climbed a mountain before, you'll have no idea what to do even if you have all the best equipment in the world. so in most cases, you'll need to learn some new skills.

    Then, you need to get to the mountain you want to climb. You need transportation to the foot of the mountain. After that, there will be logdging and food. You might want to hire a porter to lighten the load you carry up the mountain.

    Anyway, the important point in this analogy is that there's a practical side to pursuing your purpose and dream. We're here to help with that too.

    And this is the path to a truly abundant and meaningful life. One that can be full of freedom, creative expression, useful work/service and, yes, wealth in all its forms -- spiritual, intellectual and of course, material.


    Our Team

    In fact, you've spent your life chasing after the very things you didn't really want and none of the things you REALLY DID want. You were dupped. Tricked. Ahhh, it's so easy to be seduced and diverted. It takes persistance, humility and honesty to find your purpose and then focus to stay on course.

    But there is no more important journey or one that will bring you more happiness (and prosperity in every way) than finding and pursuing your purpose. Trust me.

    I knew my purpose in a general sense years before I finally embraced it and decided to seriously pursue it. It's our human nature.

    And lastly... The Name

    7 Degrees South refers to being being 7 degrees under. When you're in the minus column of life, underwater or you are starting out-- when you're not even AT it, but-- far BEHIND the Starting Line.

    7 Degrees South also refers being physically at 7 degrees south of the equator -- which is where much of this site has been produced. And importantly..

    7 Degrees South refers to a quality of seeing things from a different perspective, having a different or heterodox approach to life and problem solving.

    It also struck me as just catchy and memorable. I hope you agree.
